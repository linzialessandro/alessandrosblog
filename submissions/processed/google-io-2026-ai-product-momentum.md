At Google I/O 2026, Google CEO Sundar Pichai delivered an opening keynote highlighting a monumental shift in the artificial intelligence landscape. Ten years after pivoting Google to be an AI-first company, the focus has transitioned from experimental capabilities to tangible, daily user value. Pichai laid out Google’s full-stack approach to innovation, showing how custom silicon, advanced research, and global platforms have combined to ignite the "agentic Gemini era."

## AI Momentum Across the Full Stack

The scale of Google’s AI adoption is best measured by the sheer volume of data processed by its models. Pichai shared a striking metric that serves as a proxy for real-world adoption: monthly token processing. Tokens represent the fundamental units of data our models process, and their growth indicates how many real problems are being solved at scale.

The progression is staggering:
- In May 2024, Google processed **9.7 trillion tokens** per month across its surfaces.
- By May 2025, that number had grown to roughly **480 trillion tokens**.
- Today, in May 2026, the volume has jumped to over **3.2 quadrillion tokens** per month, representing a massive 7x year-over-year increase.

This massive scale reflects not just consumer usage, but deep integration by developers and enterprises. Over **8.5 million developers** build new applications and experiences monthly using Google’s model APIs. On average, these model APIs process approximately **19 billion tokens per minute**. In the enterprise sector, over 375 Google Cloud customers have each processed more than one trillion tokens over the past 12 months, highlighting robust commercial demand.

## Transforming Consumer Products

Google’s portfolio includes 13 products that serve over 1 billion users each, with five of those reaching more than 3 billion users. The integration of Gemini has significantly increased user engagement across these platforms.

Google Search remains the largest driver of this generative AI transition. AI Overviews now reach over **2.5 billion monthly active users**, bringing synthesized AI answers to searches worldwide. Meanwhile, the new "AI Mode," which represents Search’s largest upgrade in history, has surpassed **1 billion monthly active users** within a year of its release. Pichai noted that when users interact with AI-powered features, they use Search more, turning search sessions into multi-turn, ongoing conversations.

The Gemini app itself continues to experience exponential growth. Over the last year, its monthly active users doubled from 400 million to over **900 million**, while daily requests surged over sevenfold. Customization features, such as Personal Intelligence, have made the assistant more tailored, and more than **50 billion images** have been generated globally using the Nano Banana image generation models.

## Natural, Conversational AI Integration

Google is also expanding how naturally users can interact with AI across its services. Maps recently received its biggest update in a decade with "Ask Maps," allowing users to ask longer, more complex questions about locations.

This conversational capability is now expanding to YouTube. The new "Ask YouTube" feature reimagines video search and consumption. Instead of browsing through long videos, users can ask questions and have the AI synthesize the answers, with the video player jumping directly to the most relevant segment.

Through a unified, full-stack strategy spanning custom Tensor Processing Units (TPUs) to consumer platforms, Google is proving that the scale of AI utility is no longer a future promise, but a quadrillion-token reality.
