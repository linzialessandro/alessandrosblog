    Behind the Architecture

    Vibe Coding a Premium Web Experience

    Alessandro Linzi

    Udine

    We believe that a digital space should be as quiet, focused, and elegantly crafted as a pristine sheet of paper. This document outlines our journey of building a beautifully static, privacy-obsessed web experience from scratch. By adopting a "vibe coding" philosophy, we didn't just assemble a blog—we used this opportunity to learn the intricacies of modern programming, studying best practices to build and maintain a truly premium project.

    Standing on the Shoulders of Giants

    While modern AI tools have dramatically lowered the barrier to entry, empowering less expert individuals to build sophisticated software, we never forget the foundation we stand upon. These remarkable technologies are available today only because of the open source community and the decades of meticulous, brilliant work by incredibly skilled engineers.

    We deeply value their contributions. As we "vibe code" this platform—letting intuition and aesthetic goals guide our prompts—we do so with immense respect for the rigorous engineering principles established by those who came before us.

    Engineering Simplicity with Google AI

    Building something elegantly simple in 2026 is surprisingly complex. We aimed for absolute minimalism: no heavy frameworks, no dynamic servers, and zero trackers. Just pure semantic HTML, a refined CSS design system, and an incredibly efficient static architecture.

    To achieve this, we partnered exclusively with Google AI, leveraging the power of Gemini via Google AI Studio and the autonomous agentic capabilities of the Antigravity suite—all at the cost of a standard Google AI Pro subscription.

    The Global Project Architecture

    The codebase is designed as a modular, build-free client connected to a statically compiled API. Here is how the ecosystem functions:

    • The Source of Truth (posts.json): A single, structured JSON document containing the raw markdown, metadata, and configuration for every article.
    • Static API Engine (api/): To ensure instantaneous page loads, a Node.js compiler splits the main JSON data into a lightweight index (api/index.json) for the landing page and individual, lazy-loaded post files (api/posts/*.json) retrieved on demand.
    • Semantic Router (main.js): A client-side router that intercepts browser navigation and leverages modern View Transitions API to morph the page fluidly between post listings and reading layouts without full-page reloads.
    • Verification Engine (blogq): A custom Python package developed with Antigravity that acts as our gatekeeper, checking every post for security vulnerabilities, syntax consistency, slug cleanliness, and links safety before it can be committed.
    • Premium Offline Formats (build-pdfs.mjs): An automated serverless pipeline that compiles our articles into beautifully typeset, printable PDF documents.

    Infrastructure & Independent Domain Registry

    Consistent with our philosophy of quiet, independent digital spaces, we avoid the complexity and constraints of mainstream commercial domain registries. The blog's online presence is anchored on the free and open web, utilizing a domain name provided by the historic EU.org / nic.eu.org registry.

    By relying on this community-driven infrastructure—which has championed free domain names for non-commercial users since 1996—we pay homage to the original, decentralized ideals of the internet.

    Our Philosophy

    The core mission of this space is to make the complex intuitive, balancing high aesthetics with uncompromising performance. We build on three principles:

    1. Privacy by Design. You will find no cookie banners here, because there are no cookies. We don't track you.
    2. Performance as Respect. Your time is invaluable. There are no dynamic build steps between our writing and your reading—just a seamless, fluid static delivery.
    3. Open by Default. The engine behind this blog is entirely open source, available for you to inspect, dissect, and learn from.

    Designing the Future

    This space is more than an archive of our thoughts. It's a blueprint for yours.

    As the web pivots back toward personal publishing—away from walled gardens—we invite you to take this foundation and build upon it. Clone the repository, study the architecture, run the blogq validator on your own ideas, and explore the possibilities of vibe coding alongside AI.

    Welcome to the pursuit of better.
